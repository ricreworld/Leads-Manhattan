Reworld™ Profiled Waste HHV & Revenue Impact Calculator — How It Works

An internal decision tool (single offline HTML file — no internet, no install). It answers one question for any facility: is a profiled-waste deal worth it once you account for the MSW it displaces from the boiler? High-BTU profiled waste pays premium tip fees, but it also burns hot — every ton of it pushes ordinary MSW out of the pit. This tool nets the two against each other, per facility, per stream.

1 · The core idea — heat is the constraint, not tons
A boiler is permitted and engineered around heat input (HHV), not tonnage. If MSW burns at ~5,500 BTU/lb and a profiled waste stream burns at 10,400 BTU/lb, each profiled ton consumes the thermal room of ~1.9 MSW tons. The revenue question is therefore never "what does the profiled ton pay?" — it's "what does it pay minus the MSW revenue it squeezes out?" That displacement math is what this calculator automates.

3 · Worked example — Haverhill, default assumptions
TMW: 50 TPD × 22 delivery days = 1,100 tons at 7,250 BTU/lb, $160/ton+$176,000
Heat added (1,100 t × 7,250) displaces 1,450 tons of 5,500-BTU MSW at $42/ton−$60,900
Net monthly impact  ·  ×12 = $1,381,200 / year+$115,100

The stream is still strongly worth taking — but roughly a third of the headline tip-fee revenue is given back through displaced MSW. That giveback is invisible without this calculation, and it's the difference between pricing a deal right and overestimating it.

5 · How to use it
Open the file in any browser.Fully offline; all entries auto-save on the device. Global assumptions (30 processing days, 22 delivery days) are editable at the top.
Pick a facility card — or add one.Five plants are pre-loaded with specs, MSW price/HHV/TPD, benefits and watch-outs from the facility HHV spreadsheets. Every value is editable.
Add the profiled-waste stream being negotiated.Choose from the built-in stream library (pharma, TMW, RMW, plastics… with reference BTU values) or type your own HHV, price and TPD.
Read the verdict.Each card shows PW revenue, displaced MSW tons, lost MSW revenue, and the net monthly/annual impact — recalculated live as you negotiate price or volume. Capacity guardrails flag when PW tonnage exceeds the facility's stated headroom.
Export.Print / Save-as-PDF for the deal file, or Save-as-Excel.
