Reworld™ Verified Impact Report — How It Works

A single offline HTML file — no internet, no install, nothing leaves the laptop. You enter (or drop in) a customer's waste deliveries; it produces a customer-ready sustainability impact report. Every greenhouse-gas figure is built on Reworld's third-party-verified Lifecycle Assessment (Sept 2024 · ISO 14040/44 + ISO 14067 · verified by WAP Sustainability Consulting).

1 · The logic pipeline — inputs → engine → outputs
Inputs
Facility selectorAll 26 Reworld plants + custom. Sets net kWh/ton (Hempstead 550 verified), grid factor, steam lb/ton (Indianapolis 4,500), annual throughput.
Waste deliveriesTyped by hand, or drag-and-drop a Reworld delivery .xlsx / .csv — generator, dates, profile #, waste type and tons auto-populate; period (month / quarter / YTD) auto-detected.
Waste-type matchingEach row's "Waste Type" text is keyword-matched to one of 16 streams (pharma, plastics, RMW/TMW, paper, food, soil… default = MSW). Every factor is editable.
SettingsGWP timeframe (20-yr / 100-yr) · GHG basis (blended / stream-specific) · per-stream counterfactual (landfill vs incineration).
➜
Calculation engine
Energy pathkWh/ton = facility kWh × (BTU/lb ÷ 5,000). Hotter waste ⇒ more electricity. BTU defaults come from Reworld's own BTU Calculator reference (mixed plastic 10,250 · rubber 13,500 · food 2,600…).
GHG path — Reworld LCAPer ton, vs landfill:
+landfill methane avoided +net grid electricity +steam +metals −fossil stack CO₂ −biogenic CO₂ balance. (Full equation in §2.)
Counterfactual switchRegulated waste (pharma, RMW/TMW, solvents) can't legally go to landfill — it's compared to incineration without energy recovery: stack CO₂ is a wash, so Reworld's energy + metal recovery is a pure net gain.
Metals & equivalenciesMetal credit 0.086 t CO₂e per 50 lbs (WARM v16 via LCA). Energy translated to homes powered (893 kWh/mo), coal (1,881 kWh/ton), fuel oil (13.108 kWh/gal).

Net = 2.4 t CO₂e avoided per ton of MSW (20-yr GWP)  ·  0.65 at 100-yr GWP — both are Reworld's certified published factors, and the tool reproduces them exactly. Other streams re-weight these same six terms by their own biogenic content, fossil-carbon content, heating value and metal content.

Reading this table: greenhouse gas is a comparison vs the alternative, not an absolute. Biogenic streams (food, paper) score highest because landfilling them makes methane. Regulated streams are net-positive because incineration happens either way — only Reworld recovers the energy. Fossil streams (plastics, tires) are honestly GHG-negative vs landfill, and the report pairs that with the energy / volume / permanence benefits a landfill can never claim. Reworld's own verified all-stream band is −1.7 to −3.0 t CO₂e/ton (20-yr).

5 · How to use it — five steps, ~2 minutes
Open the HTML file in any browser.Works fully offline — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Entries auto-save on the device.
Pick the facility.Hempstead is the default. Selecting a plant sets its energy rate, grid factor and steam automatically — all remain editable.
Drop in the delivery spreadsheet (or type rows).Drag a Reworld delivery .xlsx onto the page — generator, deliveries and the reporting period populate themselves. Pick month / quarter / YTD if prompted.
Choose the lens.Leave Blended + 20-yr for the standard customer report (matches Reworld's published 2.4). Switch to Stream-specific when the customer ships non-MSW waste and you want the granular, defensible story.
Print / Save as PDF, or export Excel.The print view is the customer-ready report. "Reset all fields" clears everything for the next account.
6 · What is verified vs. what is modeled — say this to customers
✓ Verified (Reworld's certified LCA)
  • Net factor: 2.4 t CO₂e/ton MSW (20-yr) / 0.65 (100-yr) — reproduced exactly
  • All six source terms (methane 2.72 · stack 0.41 · biogenic 0.32 · electricity 0.34 · steam 0.03 · metals 0.086)
  • eGRID non-baseload grid factors (US / NY / IN) — from the LCA itself
  • Hempstead 550 kWh/ton · Indianapolis 4,500 lb steam/ton
  • Stream BTU values — Reworld's own BTU Calculator reference
⚠ Modeled (transparent, editable estimates)
  • Per-stream GHG splits for non-MSW streams — scaled from the verified MSW anchor using the LCA's own sensitivity levers; not separately certified per-stream LCAs
  • Other facilities' kWh/ton — scaled from nameplate MW ÷ TPD (fact sheets)
  • Every modeled value is visible and editable, so it can be replaced the moment official per-stream figures exist
Source of truth: Lifecycle Assessment of the Climate Impacts of MSW Diverted from Landfill to Reworld™ TTF (Sept 2024) — developed 2011, updated 8/25/2024, third-party verified by WAP Sustainability Consulting (ISO 14040 / 14044 / 14067). Verified net GHG factors: −2.4 t CO₂e/ton waste basis and −3.9 MT CO₂e/MWh electricity basis at 20-yr GWP (Table 39). The calculator is a self-contained file: Reworld-Verified-Impact-Report.html.
